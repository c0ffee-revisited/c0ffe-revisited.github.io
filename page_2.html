The Job

Short film by Tatiana Doroshenko

Mixed in 5.1 at RMIT Studios using Protools

Sound recordist Sarah Tarr supplied the atmosphere. Foley included footsteps, clothes rustling, air conditioning, "bag" sounds, doors, chairs being thrown (literally/physically/safely)!
