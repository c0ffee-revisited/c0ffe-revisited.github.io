Unity Project - Crash Material @ The Tote

This is a gif taken from a game design exercise completed in mid 2022. Building (Coding using C++) a virtual live music experience where a sound source and sound space have been mixed using Unity's 3D spacial mixer. Based on your location and camera view you can experience panning, reverb blend, base build up in the back corners of the room, the transition from room sound to direct sound when close to the'screen'. A very cool 2 minute experience that I hope to share - after figuring out how to export and embed the web version properly!
